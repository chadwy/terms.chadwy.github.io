Chadwy Terms

Service Agreement

1. General Terms

  • 1.1. The Contractor begins work only after receiving a 25% advance payment of the total project cost.
  • 1.2. Full payment means the Client accepts the work as delivered and has no claims.

2. Payment and Refund Policy

  • 2.1. If the Client cancels the project: The advance payment is non-refundable (compensation for time and resource reservation).
  • 2.2. If the Contractor cancels the project: 80% of the advance payment is refunded (20% is retained as cost compensation).
  • 2.3. After full payment and project completion: The Contractor is not obligated to make edits, fix errors or maintain the project. All Contractor obligations are considered fully completed.

3. Scope of Work (SOW)

  • 3.1. Work is performed strictly within the SOW agreed before project start.
  • 3.2. Any changes or additions not specified in the SOW are paid separately.
  • 3.3. If the Client requests changes not included in the SOW and refuses to pay: The Contractor may terminate the agreement without refunding the advance payment.

4. Project Completion and No Obligations

  • 4.1. Full payment = end of cooperation: The Client confirms receiving exactly what was ordered with no claims. The Contractor is not responsible for further functionality, modifications or project support.
  • 4.2. After project completion the Contractor is not obligated to: Respond to Client requests. Make any changes, even if they seem minor to the Client.

5. Violations and Agreement Termination

  • 5.1. The Contractor may cancel the project without refund if the Client: Requests free changes not included in SOW. Violates payment deadlines or fails to provide required data. Acts in bad faith (e.g. insists on 'minor edits' without payment).

6. Responsibilities

  • 6.1. The Contractor is not responsible for: Issues caused by changes made by Client or third parties after project completion. Malfunctions if Client refused to pay for necessary modifications.
  • 6.2. The Client must: Provide required materials and information on time. Pay for all additional work not included in SOW.

7. Final Provisions

  • 7.1. All disputes are resolved through negotiations.
  • 7.2 The Contractor may terminate the project with 80% refund if: Client violates information submission deadlines. Circumstances arise making project execution impossible.
